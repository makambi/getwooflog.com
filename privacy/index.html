Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-24

Who we are

WoofLog is developed by Vitalii Nechypor, operating as an individual based in the Netherlands. For questions about this policy or how your data is handled, contact support@getwooflog.com.

What data WoofLog processes

WoofLog is a local-first iOS app for logging your dog's health. All data you enter — pet profile information, health log entries, medication records, photos — is stored only on your device. Nothing is transmitted to our servers. We do not operate servers that receive your dog's health data.

The app does not:

  • Collect your email, name, or other personal identifiers
  • Transmit any data off your device
  • Integrate analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Share data with any third party

Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

Because WoofLog stores data only on your device and does not transmit health data off-device, there is no personal data for which we act as a controller under the GDPR in connection with your use of the app itself.

The one exception is email support: if you choose to contact us at support@getwooflog.com, the content of your email — including your email address and whatever you write — reaches our inbox. The legal basis for handling that correspondence is legitimate interest in responding to your support request. We retain support emails only for as long as needed to resolve the issue and for a reasonable period afterward in case you follow up.

Your rights under GDPR

Because we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, we cannot export or delete app data on your behalf. You have full control from within the app:

  • Access: all your data is visible in the app
  • Erasure: use "Delete All Data" in Settings, or uninstall the app
  • Portability: data export is planned for a future release
  • Objection: uninstall the app to stop all processing

For email support correspondence, you may request deletion of your emails from our inbox at any time by writing to support@getwooflog.com.

Children's privacy

WoofLog is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when app functionality changes. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be announced in the app's "What's New" on the App Store.

Contact

For any privacy questions: support@getwooflog.com. Email support is the only channel through which any personal data reaches the operator, and is retained only for the duration of the support interaction plus a reasonable follow-up window.
